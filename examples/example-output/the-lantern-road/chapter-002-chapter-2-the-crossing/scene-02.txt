The camera rides close and handheld on the bridge as a plank cracks and drops away beneath her, then whips from her white-knuckled grip up to her face as she freezes, one boot swinging over churning brown water. Holding tight on her clenched jaw, it stays with her as she breathes through her teeth, "Almost there. Almost," and feels out the next board with her dangling foot. As she lunges the last step, the camera pulls back and widens, following her stumble onto the far bank where she collapses into the mud, gasping up into the lens. Harsh noon daylight, wet sheen on everything, as [a sharp wooden crack and splash cut through the roaring current, her breath rasps, then breaks into breathless laughter as mud squelches].
